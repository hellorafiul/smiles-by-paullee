We're taking precautions to protect your health

We already exceed sterilization standards, including eliminating nearly all infectious viruses and bacteria.

  • Personalized arrival procedures to guide you from your car directly to treatment rooms to eliminate contacting surfaces
  • Maintain distancing in the reception area for essential caregivers and parents of minors if they cannot wait in a vehicle or outside the clinic
  • We request all patients wear a mask, and we removed magazines and items that can harbor any germs from the waiting area. An automated hand sanitizer is positioned by the door.
  • Hand washing station mandatory for patients and staff before all appointments
  • Record the temperature of every patient. Team member screening daily.
  • Payment arrangements are made in advance to allow contactless exit from the appointment.
  • Enhanced operatory disinfection procedures in place to clean all surfaces after each patient
  • Ambient air management with HEPA air filtration in treatment rooms and common areas to remove germs from circulating air systems
  • Enhanced HVAC system disinfection with UV germicidal light and MERV 13 filtration
  • New Aerosol vacuums to reduce the spread of bacteria during procedures
  • New personal protection equipment like face shields, gowns, and respirator masks for our doctors and team to provide barriers against even the smallest germs
  • Introduced protocols to reduce airborne aerosols during all dental procedures
  • Longer appointment times and fewer appointments available to give time between appointments for no overlap between patients
